What is the long form?

Long-form journalism is a branch of journalism dedicated to longer articles with larger amounts of content. ... Long-form articles often take the form of creative nonfiction or narrative journalism

What are short forms of writing?

short-form writing - Computer Definition. The limits imposed on the length of a single text message (160 characters) or a tweet (140 characters). It also refers to brief posts on Facebook and blogs.
